About Brewery Finder App

The Brewery Finder App allows users to search for breweries across the United States. You can search by selecting a state, entering a postal code, or both. Additionally, you can filter the search results by the type of brewery, such as microbreweries, brewpubs, or large breweries.

After performing a search, a list of breweries will be displayed, each with a link to its location on Google Maps and brewery webpage. This makes it easy to find and visit breweries near you or in specific areas.

This app is powered by the OpenBreweryDB API, which provides data on breweries across the country.
