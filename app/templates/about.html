About Brewery Finder

The Brewery Finder allows users to search for breweries across the United States. You can search by selecting a state, entering a postal code, or both. Additionally, you can filter the search results by the type of brewery, such as microbreweries, brewpubs, or large breweries.

After performing a search, a list of breweries will be displayed, each with a link to its location on Google Maps and brewery webpage. This makes it easy to find and visit breweries near you or in specific areas.

This app is powered by the OpenBreweryDB, which provides data on breweries across the country. openbrewerydb is a Python wrapper for the Open Brewery DB API. It allows for easy access to brewery data from OpenBreweryDB.
